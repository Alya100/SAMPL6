Method:
The alchemical free energy calculations were performed using the Hamiltonian Replica Exchange method.
First, ligand charges were annihilated and then van der Waals were decoupled using a linear protocol
with spacing Dlambda=0.05. A total of 92 windows were used (41 for the complex and 41 for the ligand
in solution). A harmonic restraint was used in the complex phase to keep the ligand in the binding
pocket, and an analytical correction was applied to release the ligand at the standard concentration.

Each iteration of the algorithm consisted of 500 steps of Langevin dynamics (BAOAB splitting) with a 2fs
time step, followed by a replica mixing stage. The latter was performed using a Gibbs sampling scheme
attempting 6 million replica swaps every iteration. All simulations run for 15000 iterations (i.e., 15ns per replica).
